Hello! Thank you for stopping by!

Here are a few answers to common questions:

Do you do weddings? I do not do full set up weddings. I do a-la carte weddings, where you order off a menu and I deliver to the location (or you can pick up) but no set up or breakdown is involved.

What are your delivery days? Tuesday and Friday at the moment.

Can you make me a specially styled arrangement to look like a photo I provide? Unfortunately not. I am an organic flower farm and I create garden style arrangements with seasonal flowers.

Can I buy flowers by the stem? Sadly no
